Dancer, the painter

a personal research in progress

In dance improvisations, a very common task is "to imagine drawing a line in the space with the arms". In virtual reality this task can become a real experience, with already existing open-source applications such as Gravity Sketch 3D. So, I went ahead and tried to explore movement wearing a VR headset.

Movement sentences

A movement sentence is a sequence of movements that has a clear beginning and an ending. I try to explore how movement sentences can look like in this visual world.

Outlook

As someone, who one may say is obssesed with the "body", I am fascinated by the followong questions: What happens if moving becomes merely this visual experience?
Moving without body awareness? How much body awareness does one have if one can't see their own body?
What's the meaning of moving in a virtuality, where the relationship between body and gravity is different or doesn't existence?
