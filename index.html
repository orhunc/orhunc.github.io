About me

Born in Izmir and raised in Istanbul, where I graduated from the Austrian St. Georgs - High School and attended the Conservatory for Contemporary Dance in Mimar Sinan University of Fine Arts. With the goal of pursuing a Bachelor’s in Computer Science, I moved to Berlin in 2018, where I kept training as a dancer and exploring as a performer: I have been part of multiple dance pieces, film/video projects and a drag performer. Currently, I am working as a backend developer at Augmented Robotics, a start-up that develops augmented-reality applications.

My expertise and experience as a developer lie in Android development – both in Unity and Android Studio, backend development using Bluetooth® protocols, XR development in Unity. As a dancer and performer, I am trained as a contemporary dancer and furthermore explore the art form of drag as gender performance.

As a creative technologist, I am interested in the intersection of these seemingly contradictory disciplines and exploring the choreographic/artistic material that arises by this intersection.
